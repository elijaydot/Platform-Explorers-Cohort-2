## What Microsoft Copilot Is — and Isn’t

Microsoft Copilot is a built‑in assistant that helps you work with the tools you already use such as Word, Excel, Outlook, Teams, and PowerPoint, by turning natural language into practical help.  

It doesn’t replace your thinking; it supports it by summarizing, drafting, analyzing, and organizing information right where your work already lives.  

Copilot depends on **your identity** (who you are in Microsoft Entra ID), so it knows *which* Copilot you are allowed to use.  

It depends on **your data** such as emails, files, chats, meetings, but only the ones stored in Microsoft 365 and relevant to your work.  

Most importantly, it depends on **permissions**: Copilot can only see what you are already allowed to see, nothing more.  

Copilot is **not** an all‑knowing AI and does not browse the internet freely unless the experience explicitly allows it.  

It is **not** a data leak or surveillance tool; it does not expose your information to other people or organizations.  

And it is **not** magic, as some woudl say, it works best when you give it clear context and treat it like a thoughtful, capable colleague rather than a replacement for judgment.
