## Copilot Free, Copilot Pro, and Microsoft 365 Copilot — what is the difference?

**Copilot Free** is the everyday entry point: it lets anyone chat with Copilot for basic help like brainstorming, explaining concepts, or drafting rough text outside of work files.  
It’s best for people who are curious, learning, or occasionally need help thinking things through.

**Copilot Pro** builds on that by offering faster responses and deeper help for individuals who create a lot, most especially with writing, coding, or image generation.  
It suits freelancers, creators, power users, or anyone who wants more consistent, higher‑quality AI assistance without tying it to company data.

**Microsoft 365 Copilot** is designed for work.  
It lives inside Word, Excel, PowerPoint, Outlook, and Teams, and works directly with your emails, meetings, documents, and data. It respects your identity and permissions.  
This version is best for organizations and professionals who want Copilot to help with real tasks like summarizing meetings, analyzing spreadsheets, drafting emails, or preparing presentations.

In summary: **Free** is for exploration, **Pro** is for personal productivity at scale, and **Microsoft 365 Copilot** is for doing real work, securely, inside your organization.
